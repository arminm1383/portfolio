Projects

Cashew, 2025

Cashew is an intuitive loan application app that allows users to apply for and manage micro-loans, specifically targeted towards empowering women-led small businesses in the Philippines. Our team helped to execute our project partner's vision, aiding in his goal of growing his small business all while sparking financial innovation and independence in the region.

Avi, 2025

With the natural overbearing stress that comes with car accident emergencies, Avi aims to provide drivers with immediate access, assistance, and tips, delivering an innovative design that makes use of preexisting head-up display systems. In an effort to redefine how users navigate uncertainty, our team underwent a weekend-long UX sprint, in which we researched our market, created a launch strategy, and designed a system mockup in Figma.
